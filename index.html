Why Archive Securely?

Whether you are documenting protests or just want to make sure your cat videos will live online forever, secure, distributed preservation has your back.

Features

Currently available for Android, highlights include adding metadata and Creative Commons licensing
to audiovisual mobile media and sending it to the Internet Archive over Tor.

  • Distributed Backends

    You can ensure your media is protected by creating multiple copies on different secure servers. Whether hosting locally and remote, or having your media stored on two or more remote servers, your data is encrypted and only accessible by you and who you choose to share it with.

  • Secure Transit

    By using Tor (The Onion Router), we ensure that no one can intercept your media as it travels from your phone to the archive.
